## üé® Store Customization Options

### 1. **Branding & Visual Identity**

Go to: **Dashboard ‚Üí Branding Tab**

#### Avatar/Logo
- Upload your profile picture or brand logo
- Appears as a circular avatar at the top of your store
- **Tip:** Use a square image (500x500px minimum) for best results

#### Banner Image
- Your store's hero background image
- **Recommended size:** 2000x600px
- **Tip:** Choose an image that represents your brand or genre

#### Display Name
- Your professional or pen name
- This is what visitors see (not your username)
- **Example:** "Sarah J. Maas" instead of "sarahjm123"

#### Bio
- Tell your story in 2-3 sentences
- Appears below your name on your store
- **Tip:** Include your specialties and what makes your work unique

---

### 2. **Store Appearance**

#### Theme Color
- Your brand's primary color
- Used for accents, buttons, and highlights
- **Tip:** Pick a color that matches your genre:
  - üîÆ Fantasy: Purple, Mystical Blue
  - üíñ Romance: Rose, Soft Pink
  - üïµÔ∏è Thriller: Deep Red, Dark Blue

#### Accent Color  
- Secondary color for variety
- Used sparingly for highlights
- **Tip:** Choose a complementary color to your theme

#### Font Style
Choose from:
- **Serif** - Classic, elegant (great for literary fiction)
- **Sans-Serif** - Modern, clean (perfect for contemporary)
- **Display** - Bold, artistic (ideal for fantasy/sci-fi)

#### Header Layout
- **Standard** - Traditional layout with all elements
- **Minimal** - Clean, focuses on content
- **Hero** - Large, dramatic banner (80% viewport height)

---

### 3. **Content & Messaging**

#### Welcome Message
- Custom greeting for your visitors
- Default: "Welcome to [Your Name]'s official store"
- **Examples:**
  - "Step into worlds of magic and adventure"
  - "Where stories come alive"
  - "Premium design assets for creatives"

#### Social Links
Add your social media profiles:
- **Twitter** - Share updates and engage with readers
- **Instagram** - Visual storytelling and behind-the-scenes
- **Website** - Your personal blog or portfolio
- **Custom** - Any other link you want to share

## üìö Adding Products

### Option 1: From Your Store Page
Click **"Add Product"** in the welcome banner

### Option 2: From Dashboard
1. Go to **Dashboard** (navbar)
2. Click **"Products"** or **"My Works"** tab
3. Click **"Create New"** button

### Product Information
- **Title** - Your book/asset name
- **Description** - Detailed overview (supports formatting)
- **Cover Image** - Eye-catching thumbnail (800x1200px for books)
- **Genre** - Help readers find your work
- **Type** - eBook, Asset, or Bundle
- **Price** - In your local currency
- **License** - Personal or Commercial (for assets)

### Publishing
- **Draft Mode** - Work privately until ready
- **Published** - Makes your product visible to everyone
- Use the toggle to switch between states

## üéØ Pro Tips for Success

### 1. **First Impressions Matter**
   - Upload a professional avatar
   - Choose a stunning banner image
   - Write a compelling bio

### 2. **Consistent Branding**
   - Pick 2-3 colors and stick to them
   - Use the same font family across all materials
   - Keep your visual style cohesive

### 3. **Show Your Personality**
   - Your welcome message should reflect your voice
   - Add social links to connect beyond the store
   - Update your bio as you grow

### 4. **Quality Over Quantity**
   - Start with 1-2 excellent products
   - High-quality covers attract more clicks
   - Detailed descriptions convert browsers to buyers

### 5. **Keep It Fresh**
   - Update your banner seasonally
   - Refresh your welcome message periodically
   - Add new products regularly
